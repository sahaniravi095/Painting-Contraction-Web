STENCILS PAINTING SERVICE
Stencilling creates a pattern or an image through the application of pigment to a surface over a median object with 
designed gaps in it. It further enables to shape the image or pattern by resourcing the pigment to reach fewer areas 
of the surface. Practically, the stencil is a thin sheet of element, such as wood, paper or metal with design or 
letters shaped from the same, and further utilising it to create design or letters on the primary surface by applying 
pigment through the cut-out holes in the element.
